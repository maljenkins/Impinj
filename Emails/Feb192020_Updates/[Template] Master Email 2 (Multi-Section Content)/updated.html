RAIN RFID is your key to Industry 4.0
RAIN RFID is a passive (battery-free) form of radio frequency tracking that connects millions of items to the internet. RAIN RFID is used in applications such as inventory management, asset tracking, and item authentication and has the ability to:
Uniquely identify individual items
Identify 1000's of items per second
Identify item location
